Deep underneath the Inn is what the previous owner calls a basement. Dark and musty, it makes a great wine cellar, as well as a breeding ground for vermin.
A large rock outcropping with several underground caves stands in a sparse woodland. This makes a perfect gathering spot for various forest creatures, and possibly other creatures.
This large meadow within a dense forest is filled with clover flowers. A great place for creatures to lure overzealous heroes.
Deep shadaws stretch across this large, tandled oak thicket, hiding all manner of creatures.
